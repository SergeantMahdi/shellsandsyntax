Instructions for Playing the Game

Step 1: Observe the Shell

  • A shell image appears in the center of the screen.
  • Examine its shape, color, and texture to help identify it.

Step 2: Review the Answer Choices

  • Three possible names for the shell will appear below the image.
  • Read each name carefully before making a selection.

Step 3: Drag the Correct Answer

  • Click and hold the name you think matches the shell.
  • Drag it toward the drop zone (the empty box under the image).

Step 4: Drop Your Answer

  • Release your mouse (or lift your finger on a touchscreen) to drop the name into the box.
  • The game will check if your answer is correct.

Step 5: Check Your Result

  • If correct: A message appears saying "Correct" in green text.
    • The game automatically loads the next shell after a short delay.
  • If incorrect: A message appears saying "Oops! Try again!" in red text.
    • You can drag another name to try again.

Step 6: Continue Playing

  • The game loops through different shells.
  • Keep playing to test your knowledge and discover more shell species!
