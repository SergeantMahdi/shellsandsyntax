About Shells from Around the World

It's a fun and interactive game that invites you to explore shells from different parts of the globe. Discover and match shells to their names, uncover unique species, and challenge your knowledge of land and marine life. Created to be both educational and enjoyable, this game brings the beauty of shells to your fingertips. Ready to dive in?

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an educational yet entertaining platform for anyone interested in shells, from beginners to advanced collectors. We aim to make learning about shells accessible and fun for everyone.

Key Features

  • Drag-and-drop game to match shells with their names

About the Creator

Created by Christabel, a passionate newbie coder with a love for programming, while the stunning shell photos are the work of my husband, Andrea Nappo aka Nappus, a seashell enthusiast with a deep passion for malacology. Together, we have combined our interests to create a platform that celebrates both the beauty of marine life and the art of beginner coding. It's a fusion of our shared love for nature and technology, bringing the fascinating world of shells to life.
